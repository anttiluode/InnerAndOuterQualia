the living view the engine, as an eigensolver

This is the same dreaming-field engine from the series, run as an eigensolver: it surfaces the modes your images are built from, strongest first, then climbs the spectrum. It cannot show anything that isn't already in your images.

What this is: principal eigenmodes of your image set (PCA on the pixel covariance), computed via the snapshot trick and rendered. The value is convenience, privacy, and the dominance readout — not new math. What it isn't: a claim about consciousness. The qualia interpretation that motivates the wider project is the bet, and nothing here touches it. This is the mechanism being useful on its own terms.
